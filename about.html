What is BusMall?

Busmall is a Seattle transit system of buses to help you get from where you are to where you're going, while providing our commuters with practical and relevant products so you can do your shopping while you commute! We are currently trying to get a grasp of what our customer's wants/needs are so please take a look at our home page and give us your opinions by voting on what products we should carry! We hope to one day provide you with all your needs so that you don't have to make a separate trip for your daily/weekly shopping needs! Jump on board and pick up your groceries or knick knacks while you make your commute home or to work. There's no need to go shopping anymore!
